# Asset Credits

## 3D Models

All 3D models are from **Kenney** (https://kenney.nl) and licensed under **CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain)**.

### Vehicle Models
- **Toy Car Kit** - https://kenney.nl/assets/toy-car-kit
  - `vehicle-truck.glb` - Used for Standard truck
  - `vehicle-monster-truck.glb` - Used for Heavy truck
  - `vehicle-racer.glb` - Used for Fast truck
  - `tree.glb` - Environment decoration
  - `tree-pine.glb` - Environment decoration

### Road Assets
- **City Kit (Roads)** - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-roads
  - Road segment models

### Building Models
- **City Kit (Commercial)** - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-commercial
  - `building-a.glb` through `building-d.glb` - City buildings
